PonyWalls is an Android application to view, download and share My Little Pony: Friendship is Magic wallpapers. It is based on RedditReader by Kanav Gandhi.
It allows users to view wallpapers, download and share them, and set them as wallpaper. The wallpapers are fetched from reddit.
And the best thing: PonyWalls is completely free and open source software. I want to give back my thank to the FOSS community, which influenced my life in a lot of ways.

Current Version: Pre-Alpha (no release)
